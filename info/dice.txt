There are 6 dice used in Orlog with 5 possible symbols on each side. Every die also has 2 sides that have a dotted outline indicating a gain of god favor tokens. This differs for each die. Here is a list of all dice with their configurations as seen in the the game.

List:
2 axe - 1 arrow - 1 helmet (dotted) - 1 shield (dotted) - 1 hand
2 axe - 1 arrow - 1 helmet (dotted) - 1 shield - 1 hand (dotted)
2 axe - 1 arrow - 1 helmet - 1 shield (dotted) - 1 hand (dotted)
2 axe - 1 arrow (dotted) - 1 helmet (dotted) - 1 shield - 1 hand
2 axe - 1 arrow (dotted) - 1 helmet - 1 shield - 1 hand (dotted)
